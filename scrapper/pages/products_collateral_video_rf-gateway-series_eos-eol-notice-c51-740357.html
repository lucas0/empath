End-of-Life and End-of-Sale Notices End-of-Sale and End-of-Life Announcement for the Cisco RF Gateway 10 Hardware Save Download Print Updated:February 23, 2018 Document ID:1519420990325764 Bias-Free Language EOL12311 Cisco announces the end-of-sale and end-of-life dates for the Cisco RF Gateway 10 Hardware. The last day to order the affected product(s) is August 24, 2018. Customers with active service contracts or active warranties will continue to receive support from the Cisco Technical Assistance Center (TAC) as shown in Table 1 of the EoL bulletin. Table 1 describes the end-of-life milestones, definitions, and dates for the affected product(s). Table 2 lists the product part numbers affected by this announcement. For customers with active service contracts or active product warranties, support will be available for the shown dates as stated in the applicable service contracts or product warranty terms and conditions. This EOL pertains to the RFGW-10 product Family. This RFGW-10 EOL coincides with uBR10K EOL11732. See migration section below migration options. Table 1. End-of-Life Milestones and Dates for the Cisco RF Gateway 10 Hardware Milestone Definition Date End-of-Life Announcement Date The date the document that announces the end-of-sale and end-of-life of a product is distributed to the general public. February 23, 2018 End-of-Sale Date: HW The last date to order the product through Cisco point-of-sale mechanisms. The product is no longer for sale after this date. August 24, 2018 Last Ship Date: HW The last-possible ship date that can be requested of Cisco and/or its contract manufacturers. Actual ship date is dependent on lead time. November 22, 2018 End of Routine Failure Analysis Date: HW The last-possible date a routine failure analysis may be performed to determine the cause of hardware product failure or defect. August 24, 2019 End of New Service Attachment Date: HW For equipment and software that is not covered by a service-and-support contract, this is the last date to order a new service-and-support contract or add the equipment and/or software to an existing service-and-support contract. August 24, 2019 End of Service Contract Renewal Date: HW The last date to extend or renew a service contract for the product. November 19, 2022 Last Date of Support: HW The last date to receive applicable service and support for the product as entitled by active service contracts or by warranty terms and conditions. After this date, all support services for the product are unavailable, and the product becomes obsolete**. Warranty duration is based on product ship dates; refer to warranty terms and conditions for details.
