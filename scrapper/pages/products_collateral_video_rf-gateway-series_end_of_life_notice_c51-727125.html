End-of-Life and End-of-Sale Notices Change in Product Part Number Announcement for the Cisco RF Gateway 1 Power Cords Save Download Print Updated:March 6, 2013 Document ID:1518366112990721 Bias-Free Language EOL9119 Cisco announces a change in product part numbers for the Cisco RF Gateway 1 Power Cords. This is a product part number change only announcement. Customers will experience no change in functionality when using the new product part numbers. The new product part numbers are designed to simplify the customer ordering process. The last day to order the affected product using the current product part numbers is June 5, 2013. Customers will continue to receive support from the Cisco Technical Assistance Center (TAC) until otherwise specified in the end-of-life announcement for the new product part numbers.
