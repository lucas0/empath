End-of-life Milestones and Dates Milestone Definition Date End-of-Life Notice Announcement of end-of-life of the product posted on Cisco.com. Dec 9th 2019 End of Routine Maintenance This milestone indicates that from this date, Cisco will publish only fixes for security vulnerabilities and severity 1 issues pertaining to the release as determined by the Cisco ISE team. June 8th 2020 End of Software Maintenance This is the last date on which Cisco engineering may release the final software maintenance releases or bug fixes, if any. After this date, Cisco engineering will no longer develop, repair, maintain, or test the product software, including severity 1 and security vulnerabilities. June 8th 2021 End of Support This is the last date to receive applicable service and support for the product as entitled by active service contracts or by warranty terms and conditions. June 8th 2022 Product Migration Options Install and upgrade guides: https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/td/docs/security/ise/2-6/install_guide/b_ise_InstallationGuide_26.html.
