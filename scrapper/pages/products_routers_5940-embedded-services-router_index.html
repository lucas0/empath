Deploy rugged Cisco 5940 Embedded Services Routers (ESRs) for mobile and embedded networks that require IP routing and services. These routers: Are designed for use in harsh environments Have a flexible, compact form factor (about the size of a 4x6 photograph) Are complemented by Cisco IOS Software and Cisco Mobile Ready Net capabilities Cisco 5940 ESRs also provide highly secure and scalable data, voice, and video communications to stationary and mobile network nodes across wired and wireless links. Feature and Capabilities The Cisco 5940 ESR: Supports multiple applications while running concurrently over wired or wireless networks Solves critical size, weight, and power challenges Offers onboard hardware encryption to offload encryption processing from the router Provides investment protection for bandwidth-intensive applications These high-performance routers also include four Gigabit Ethernet interfaces and a comprehensive Cisco IOS Software feature set. These components help protect your investment in deploying bandwidth-intensive applications in mobile or embedded networks in markets such as: Public safety Transportation Defense Heavy construction Mining The Cisco 5940 is a 3U Compact PCI router card that is available in both air- and conduction-cooled models.
