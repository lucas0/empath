End-of-Life and End-of-Sale Notices End-of-Sale and End-of-Life Announcement for the ONS 15216 40channels Multiplexer/Demultiplexer Save Download Print Updated:January 23, 2017 Document ID:1484963881281362 Bias-Free Language EOL11403 Cisco announces the end-of-sale and end-of-life dates for the ONS 15216 40channels Multiplexer/Demultiplexer. The last day to order the affected product(s) is January 20, 2018. Customers with active service contracts will continue to receive support from the Cisco Technical Assistance Center (TAC) as shown in Table 1 of the EoL bulletin. Table 1 describes the end-of-life milestones, definitions, and dates for the affected product(s). Table 2 lists the product part numbers affected by this announcement. For customers with active and paid service and support contracts, support will be available under the terms and conditions of customers' service contract. Table 1. January 20, 2019 End of Service Contract Renewal Date: HW The last date to extend or renew a service contract for the product. April 20, 2022 Last Date of Support: HW The last date to receive applicable service and support for the product as entitled by active service contracts or by warranty terms and conditions.
