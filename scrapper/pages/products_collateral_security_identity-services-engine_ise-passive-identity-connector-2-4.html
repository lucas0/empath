Software maintenance support for 2.4 software release ends on December 26th 2021. No patches or maintenance releases will be provided for ISE-PIC 2.4 releases after that date. Starting December 26th, 2020 only sev1 and security vulnerability issues will be addressed. ISE-PIC 2.4 will reach end of support at December 26th 2022.
