Obtaining a Universally Unique Device Identifier (UUID) for Cisco Unified Communications Manager (CUCM) Step 1: In Cisco Prime License Manager, in the top right corner of the page, click About. Step result: The system displays a pop-up window. Step 2: From the pop-up window, copy the Registration ID which represents the universally unique device identifier (UUID) on the License Registration Portal (LRP).
