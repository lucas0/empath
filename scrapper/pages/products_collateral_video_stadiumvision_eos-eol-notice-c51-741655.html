End-of-Life and End-of-Sale Notices End-of-Sale and End-of-Life Announcement for the Cisco StadiumVision Director 4K DMP Series Save Download Print Updated:November 30, 2018 Document ID:1543603655010904 Bias-Free Language EOL12862 Cisco announces the end-of-sale and end-of-life dates for the Cisco StadiumVision Director 4K DMP Series. The last day to order the affected product(s) is May 31, 2019. Customers with active service contracts will continue to receive support from the Cisco Technical Assistance Center (TAC) as shown in Table 1 of the EoL bulletin. Table 1 describes the end-of-life milestones, definitions, and dates for the affected product(s). Table 2 lists the product part numbers affected by this announcement. For customers with active and paid service and support contracts, support will be available under the terms and conditions of customers' service contract. Table 1. May 30, 2020 End of Service Contract Renewal Date: HW The last date to extend or renew a service contract for the product. August 26, 2023 Last Date of Support: HW The last date to receive applicable service and support for the product as entitled by active service contracts or by warranty terms and conditions.
