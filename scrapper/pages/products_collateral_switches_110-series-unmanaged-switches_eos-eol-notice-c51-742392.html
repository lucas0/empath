End-of-Life and End-of-Sale Notices End-of-Sale and End-of-Life Announcement for the Cisco Small Business Unmanaged Switches (95 and 110 Series, select models) Save Download Print Updated:May 15, 2019 Document ID:1571735926110362 Bias-Free Language EOL13046 Cisco announces the end-of-sale Cisco Small Business Unmanaged Switches (95 and 110 Series, select models). The last day to order the affected product(s) is August 12, 2019. Customers will continue to receive phone support from the Cisco Small Business Support Center (SBSC) as shown in Table 1 of the EoL bulletin. Table 1 describes the end-of-life milestones, definitions, and dates for the affected product(s). Table 2 lists the product part numbers affected by this announcement. For customers with active product warranties, support will be available as stated in the product warranty terms and conditions. Table 1. August 12, 2019 End of Phone Support: HW The last date to receive phone support as part of the product warranty.
