Technology Support Virtual LANs/VLAN Trunking Protocol (VLANs/VTP) Virtual LAN. Group of devices on one or more LANs that are configured (using management software) so that they can communicate as if they were attached to the same wire, when in fact they are located on a number of different LAN segments.
