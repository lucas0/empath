End-of-Life and End-of-Sale Notices Cisco Identity Services Engine Software Version 2.3 Product Bulletin Save Download Print Updated:March 5, 2019 Document ID:1545329027461455 Bias-Free Language PB741734 Cisco announces the end-of-life dates for the Cisco Identity Services Engine (ISE) Software Version 2.3 software. Software maintenance support for 2.3 software releases end on December 17th 2019. No patches or maintenance releases will be provided for ISE 2.3 releases after that date. Starting June 17th 2019 only sev1 and security vulnerability issues will be addressed. ISE 2.3 will reach end of support at June 17th 2020. Software maintenance requires an active service contract. Product Migration Options Install and upgrade guides can be found here: https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/support/security/identity-services-engine/products-installation-guides-list.html. Additional Support: https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/td/docs/security/ise/2-4/release_notes/b_ise_24_rn.html#UpgradeInformation24. Customers may also need to purchase additional Cisco ISE products such as Cisco ISE Plus and Cisco ISE Apex, depending on functionality requirements. Customers are encouraged to review the Cisco ISE ordering guide and to speak with their sales representative to ensure that they migrate to the correct mix of Cisco ISE products.
