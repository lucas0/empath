The last day to order the affected product(s) is February 18, 2019. Customers with active service contracts will continue to receive support from the Cisco Technical Assistance Center (TAC) as shown in Table 1 of the EoL bulletin. Table 1 describes the end-of-life milestones, definitions, and dates for the affected product(s). Table 2 lists the product part numbers affected by this announcement. For customers with active and paid service and support contracts, support will be available under the terms and conditions of customers' service contract. Table 1. May 16, 2021 Last Date of Support : App SW The last date to receive applicable service and support for the product as entitled by active service contracts or by warranty terms and conditions.
