Revision History Revision Publish Date Comments 1.0 10-Apr-18 Initial Release Products Affected Affected Product ID Comments NCS1K-CNTLR NCS1K-CNTLR= Part Alternate Defect Information Defect ID Headline CSCvi72143 Mismatch of MFG stickers/labels in the NCS1K-CNTLR= PID Problem Description A limited number of NCS1K-CNTLR cards may have a mismatch in the product label. The units may have a label CNTLR2 as shown in this picture. Background The product label on the NCS1K-CNTLR controller (or CPU) unit might appear as CNTLR2 instead of CNTLR on a few units. Problem Symptom The product label mismatch on the NCS1K-CNTLR unit may misguide users. Workaround/Solution In order to identify the NCS1K-CNTRL correctly, see the PID VID label only for identification of the unit. How To Identify Affected Products The PID VID label must be identified as "NCS1K-CNTLR = V0X” where X refers to the version number as shown in this picture.
