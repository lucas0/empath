End-of-Life and End-of-Sale Notices End-of-Sale and End-of-Life Announcement for the Cisco ONS 15216 Fiber Bragg Grating Dispersion Compensation Units (FBG DCUs) Save Download Print Updated:September 17, 2015 Document ID:1549301651069390 Bias-Free Language EOL10539 Cisco announces the end-of-sale and end-of-life dates for the Cisco ONS 15216 Fiber Bragg Grating Dispersion Compensation Units (FBG DCUs). The last day to order the affected product(s) is September 18, 2016. Customers with active service contracts will continue to receive support from the Cisco Technical Assistance Center (TAC) as shown in Table 1 of the EoL bulletin. Table 1 describes the end-of-life milestones, definitions, and dates for the affected product(s). Table 2 lists the product part numbers affected by this announcement. For customers with active and paid service and support contracts, support will be available under the terms and conditions of customers' service contract. Table 1.
