End-of-Life and End-of-Sale Notices End-of-Sale and End-of-Life Announcement for the Cisco TelePresence Video Communication Server Licenses and Software Images for Release X6.x Save Download Print Updated:January 11, 2019 Document ID:1549302127188905 Bias-Free Language EOL9671 Cisco announces the end-of-sale and end-of-life dates for the Cisco TelePresence Video Communication Server Licenses and Software Images for Release X6.x. The last day to order the affected product(s) is September 26, 2014. Customers with active service contracts will continue to receive support from the Cisco Technical Assistance Center (TAC) as shown in Table 1 of the EoL bulletin. Table 1 describes the end-of-life milestones, definitions, and dates for the affected product(s).Table 2 lists the product part numbers affected by this announcement. For customers with active and paid service and support contracts, support will be available under the terms and conditions of customers' service contract. Table 1. December 22, 2016 Last Date of Support: App. SW The last date to receive applicable service and support for the product as entitled by active service contracts or by warranty terms and conditions.
