End-of-Life and End-of-Sale Notices End-of-Sale and End-of-Life Announcement for the Cisco Nexus 2232PP, 2232TM-E, 2332TQ, 2348TQ Fabric Extenders Updated:July 31, 2020 Bias-Free Language Contact Cisco Table of Contents Table of Contents Overview End-of-life milestones Product part numbers Product migration options For more information Overview EOL13150 - Amended Cisco announces the end-of-sale and end-of-life dates for the Cisco Nexus 2232PP, 2232TM-E, 2332TQ, 2348TQ Fabric Extenders. The last day to order the affected product(s) is September 9, 2020. Customers with active service contracts will continue to receive support from the Cisco Technical Assistance Center (TAC) as shown in Table 1 of the EoL bulletin. Table 1 describes the end-of-life milestones, definitions, and dates for the affected product(s). Table 2 lists the product part numbers affected by this announcement. For customers with active and paid service and support contracts, support will be available under the terms and conditions of customers' service contract. End-of-life milestones Table 1.
