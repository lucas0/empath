End-of-Life and End-of-Sale Notices End-of-Sale and End-of-Life Announcement for the Cisco Select CSP 2100 Accessory Updated:May 6, 2022 Bias-Free Language Table of Contents Table of Contents Overview End-of-life milestones Product part numbers Product migration options For more information Overview EOL14752 Cisco announces the end-of-sale and end-of life dates for the Cisco Select CSP 2100 Accessory. The last day to order the affected product(s) is November 4, 2022. Customers with active service contracts will continue to receive support from the Cisco Technical Assistance Center (TAC) as shown in Table 1 of the EoL bulletin. Table 1 describes the end-of-life milestones, definitions, and dates for the affected product(s). Table 2 lists the product part numbers affected by this announcement. For customers with active and paid service and support contracts, support will be available under the terms and conditions of customers' service contract. End-of-life milestones Table 1. November 4, 2023 End of Service Contract Renewal Date: HW The last date to extend or renew a service contract for the product. January 30, 2027 Last Date of Support: HW The last date to receive applicable service and support for the product as entitled by active service contracts or by warranty terms and conditions.
