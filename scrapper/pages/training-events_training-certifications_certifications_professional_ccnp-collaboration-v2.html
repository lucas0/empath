Earning your CCNP Collaboration certification proves you have what it takes to build the solutions empowering our hybrid world. Test your knowledge and skills with collaboration technologies such as the Cisco Internetwork Operations System XE gateway, call control, Quality of Service, and more on the CLCOR exam and showcase your specialty with a concentration exam of your choice. Pass both exams to earn your certification. Possible job roles Collaboration administrator Collaboration solutions engineer Collaboration solutions architect Prerequisites No formal prerequisites for CCNP collaboration. CCNP candidates often also have three to five years of experience implementing security solutions. Active Status All Cisco certifications are valid for 3 years. Recertification Pass any one professional concentration exam and earn 40 CE credits or earn 80 continuing education credits. Exams and trainings Step 1: Take the Core exam and recommended training Exam 350-801 CLCOR Test your knowledge of the core skills necessary to configure and implement key network collaboration technologies such as protocols, codecs, endpoints, call control, and additional Cisco applications. Training Implementing Cisco Collaboration Core Technologies (CLCOR) Develop the skills and knowledge necessary to implement and deploy core collaboration technologies, including infrastructure and design, and prepare to take the 350-801 CLCOR exam. Private training Step 2: Choose one Concentration exam and recommended training 300-810 CLICA Demonstrate your mastery of collaboration applications including SSO, Cisco Unified IM and presence, and Cisco Unity Connection and Cisco Unity Express. Recommended training: Implementing Cisco Collaboration Applications 300-815 CLACCM Showcase your mastery of advanced call control and mobility services, including signaling and media protocols, the Cisco Unified Board Element, call control and dial planning, and Cisco Unified CM Call Control. Recommended training: Implementing Cisco Advanced Call Control and Mobility Services 300-820 CLCEI Showcase your knowledge of collaboration cloud and edge solutions, including Cisco Unified IM, expressway configurations, and Cisco Webex Teams hybrid and emerging technologies. Recommended training: Implementing Cisco Collaboration Cloud and Edge Solutions 300-835 CLAUTO Showcase your ability to implement Cisco Collaboration automated, programmable solutions for voice, video, and conferencing on-premises or in the cloud.
